About Us

The Women safety and Empowerment, is the non-profit organization, generally concerned with advising the government on all policy matters affecting women. The objective of the Women safety and Empowerment is to represent the rights of women in India and to provide a voice for their issues and concerns. The subjects of the campaigns run by Women safety and Empowerment have included dowry, politics, religion, equal representation for women in jobs, and the exploitation of women for labour.
